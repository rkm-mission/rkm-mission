Schedule of Activities of Aashrama

The temple opens at 05.00 A.M. and closes at 12.00 noon. In the evening it opens at 04.00 P.M. and remains open until 8.30 P.M.

Daily Schedule

Mangalarati & Ushakirtana (Waving of lights & singing of devotional hymns) 05.00 A.M
Vedic Chanting & Gita Chanting 07.00 A.M
Morning Puja (RitualisticWorship) 08.15 A.M
Lalita/ Vishnu/ Shiva Sahasranama & Other Stotras Chanting 06.00 P.M
Evening Arati with Prayer & Bhajans 06.45 P.M
Yoga Classes for General Public both men & women ( Registration Fee : Rs. 600/- and subsequent months Rs. 300/- per month ) 06.00 A.M- 08.00 A.M (Daily except Sundays)

Weekly Schedule

Saturday
Upanishad Class in English 07.30 A.M
Discourses in Kannada - Bhagavad Gita 05.45 P.M - 06:30 P.M
Sunday
Discourses in English - The Gospel of Sri Ramakrishna 05.45 P.M - 06:30 P.M
Guided Meditation Class 4.00 P.M - 5.30 P.M
Balaka Sangha Class for Children aged between 10 to 13 years 8.30 A.M. - 11.30 A.M.
Taruna Sangha Class for Children aged between 14 to 18 years: Swami Vivekananda's Works 8.00 A.M. - 10.00 A.M.
Yuvaka Sangha Class for Youths aged between 19 to 35 years 3.15 P.M. - 5.15 P.M.

Library

Library services can be availed upon nominal subscription. The books include philosophy, religion, ethics and lives of great saints in English, Kannada and Sanskrit. We have about 3450 books. It is open in the evening between 4.30 PM and 6.30 PM.
